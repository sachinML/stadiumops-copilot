## Sustainability nudges (fan-facing)

- Prefer transit or park & ride when feasible to reduce last-mile emissions.
- Bring a reusable bottle and use the **Water Refill Station**.
- Sort waste at **Waste Sorting / Recycling Point** (recycle/compost/trash).

## Operations (staff-facing)

- When redirecting crowds, consider routes that avoid unnecessary backtracking.
- Use concise prompts on screens: “Refill • Reuse • Sort” to reduce contamination in waste streams.
