## Transportation + last-mile guidance

- Transit: The default recommendation is **Central Station (Transit Hub)** → East Transit Plaza → Gate B.
- Park & Ride: Use **Lot P** and walk step-free to West Parking Plaza → Gate D.
- Rideshare/taxi: Use the dedicated **Rideshare / Taxi Drop-off** → South Drop-off Plaza → Gate C.

## Peak-time mitigation tips (match day)

- Arrive early: Encourage arrival **60–90 minutes before kickoff** to reduce queuing and security bottlenecks.
- Rerouting: If a gate is congested, suggest an adjacent gate and a concourse walk inside (clockwise/counter-clockwise).
- Accessible arrivals: Recommend earlier arrival for guests requiring step-free routing or assistance to avoid peak surges.
