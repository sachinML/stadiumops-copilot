## Crowd management playbook (venue staff)

### Congestion response (15-minute window)

- Queue splitting: Use stanchions + volunteers to split queues into 2–3 lanes and prevent pinch points.
- Redirect messaging: Push a short notification that includes:
  - which gate to avoid
  - alternative gate(s)
  - “arrive prepared” checklist (tickets ready, bags policy, prohibited items)
- Accessibility protection: Ensure the accessible queue remains visible and protected from merge-in.

### Incident triage

- Minor medical / fainting: route to First Aid and reduce local density.
- Lost guest: route to Guest Services & Accessibility Desk.
- Escalation: if density is high and incidents increase, request additional stewards/security and coordinate with transport ops to stagger arrivals.
