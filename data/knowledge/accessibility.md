## Accessibility quick guide

- Step-free routes: Prefer the **Elevator Core (Step-free)** from the inner concourse when the guest uses a wheelchair, mobility aid, stroller, or requests a step-free path.
- Accessible entry: Each gate has an **accessible queue**; staff should prioritize clear signage and a dedicated volunteer at peak surges.
- Sensory support: The **Sensory Relief Room** is available for guests needing a quiet, low-stimulation space.
- Assistance: The **Guest Services & Accessibility Desk** can coordinate wheelchair assistance, hearing/vision support, and wayfinding help.

## Inclusive communication

- Use short sentences and clear landmarks (Gate + Plaza + Concourse).
- Offer options: “fastest route” vs “least crowded” vs “step-free route”.
- Confirm critical details: section, gate, and any accessibility needs.
